## What Makes Starburst Work

The core mechanic is a 5x3 reel grid with 10 paylines that pay in both directions — left to right and right to left. That double-direction pay structure effectively doubles your coverage on every spin without requiring extra complexity from the player. Wins form when three or more matching symbols land on a payline from either the leftmost or rightmost reel inward.

The RTP sits at 96.09%, which is above the South African industry average of roughly 95%. Combined with a hit frequency of 22.6%, you can expect a winning outcome on roughly one in every four to five spins. Most of those wins are small — Starburst is not a game that builds tension around rare feature triggers. It rewards patience and steady play.

## The Starburst Wild Feature

The standout feature is the Starburst Wild symbol, which appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 only. When it lands, it expands to fill the entire reel and locks in place while the remaining reels re-spin. If another Wild lands during the re-spin, that reel also locks and a further re-spin is awarded. This chain can repeat up to three times, and the best outcome — three full wild reels covering columns 2, 3, and 4 — creates the highest practical win the game can deliver, up to 500x your stake.

The expanding Wild does not substitute for the bar symbols, but it covers every other symbol type, which keeps the re-spin sequences valuable across the full paytable.
