## Core Mechanics and Features: How Starburst Works

### What Is a Starburst Slot Machine?

The Starburst slot machine is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot with 10 fixed paylines. What makes it different from the traditional slot format is the combination of **Win Both Ways** and the **Expanding Wild with Re-Spins**. These two mechanics carry the entire game. There's no bonus round. No scatter pays. No cascading wins. Just focused, clean design — and it works.

### Win Both Ways

Genuinely innovative when NetEnt introduced it. Most slots in 2012 paid left to right only. Starburst pays both directions across the same 10 paylines, which effectively gives you 20 ways to win per spin.

Land four matching gem symbols starting from the right reel and you still collect. Simple as that. But it changes the rhythm of a session quite noticeably — the dead spins where nothing connects feel shorter, and the hit frequency feels friendlier than comparable low-payline slots from the same era.

### The Starburst Expanding Wild

The centrepiece of everything. One of the best Wild mechanics in the history of online slots.

The multicoloured Starburst Wild can only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. When it lands, it expands to fill all three rows on that reel, locks in place, and triggers a Re-Spin. If another Wild lands during that Re-Spin on a different eligible reel — it also expands, locks, and triggers another. Up to three Re-Spins from a single triggering event.

Picture the sequence: Wild on reel 2 → Re-Spin → Wild on reel 4 → Re-Spin → Wild on reel 3 → final Re-Spin. Three locked Wild reels covering 9 of 15 positions, paying both ways simultaneously. The satisfaction comes precisely from how uncluttered it is — no animation overload, no cutscene, just reels doing exactly what they're supposed to.

For a full breakdown of every mechanic, read the [Starburst features guide](/features/).

### Re-Spins — Not Traditional Free Spins

A lot of South African players come in expecting a free spins bonus round. There isn't one. What Starburst has instead is the Re-Spin mechanic built directly into the Wild — which is effectively a free spins feature, just not triggered by scatters.

Worth clarifying: when a casino advertises "50 Starburst free spins," those are bonus spins credited to your account at a fixed bet value. Not a triggerable in-game feature. The Re-Spin mechanic is entirely separate — it triggers during normal play when a Wild lands.

### Symbol Payouts

**Low-Paying Symbols (Gemstones):** Purple, Blue, Red, Green, Yellow Gemstones

**High-Paying Symbols:** Lucky 7 (strongest non-Wild symbol), Gold BAR

**Wild:** Rainbow Starburst symbol — expands to cover full reel, substitutes for all other symbols

The gems pay modestly on three-of-a-kind. The 7 and BAR pay significantly better. And the Wild, even partially locked on a single reel, can transform an otherwise blank spin into something worth noting.
