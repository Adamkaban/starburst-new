## Core Mechanics Deep Dive

### Starburst Expanding Wilds: The Star of the Show

The Starburst Wild isn't just a standard substitution symbol — it's the entire engine driving this game's excitement.

Here's exactly how it works.

**What it looks like:** A multicoloured, jewel-studded star that radiates light outward across the reel. Impossible to miss.

**Where it lands:** The Starburst Wild can only appear on reels 2, 3, and 4. Never on reel 1 or reel 5. This is intentional — the middle reels are positioned to generate the most payline coverage when a full-reel wild locks in.

**What happens when it lands:**
1. The wild expands instantly to fill the entire reel — all three rows become wild
2. A re-spin triggers at no cost to you
3. The expanded wild reel locks in place while the remaining reels spin again
4. If another wild lands on a different eligible reel during the re-spin, it also expands and locks — triggering yet another re-spin
5. This can chain for a maximum of three consecutive re-spins

The expanding wilds substitute for every other symbol on the grid. With two or three wild reels locked simultaneously, you're looking at wins across multiple paylines — running in both directions, because of the Win Both Ways mechanic.

**Maximum wild scenario:** All three middle reels — 2, 3, and 4 — fill with expanding wilds simultaneously. Combined with Win Both Ways, this is where the 500x maximum payout lives.

**Re-spin chain in practice:**
- Wild lands on reel 3 → reel 3 locks, re-spin triggers
- During re-spin, wild lands on reel 2 → reel 2 locks, another re-spin triggers
- During second re-spin, wild lands on reel 4 → reel 4 locks, final re-spin triggers
- Third re-spin plays out with three wild reels in place

Three re-spins is the absolute ceiling. After the third completes, win totals are calculated and the round ends.

### Win Both Ways: Double Your Payline Power

With Starburst's win both ways mechanic, a matching combination of three or more symbols pays out regardless of which direction it runs across the payline. Left to right, right to left — both count independently.

**A practical example:**
- Three Bar symbols on reels 3, 4, and 5 → pays right to left
- Three gems on reels 1, 2, and 3 → pays left to right
- Both wins calculated and paid on the same spin

This mechanic effectively turns the 10 fixed paylines into 20 active win opportunities per spin. Combined with the expanding wilds, Win Both Ways is what gives the game its surprisingly strong hit frequency of 22.6%.

Starburst runs 10 fixed paylines across a 5-reel, 3-row grid. You cannot deactivate any of them — always on, every spin. Full payline coverage guaranteed, no accidentally missed winning combinations.

## FAQ: Starburst Features Explained

**Q: Does Starburst have free spins?**
Not in the traditional sense. There's no free spins bonus round triggered by scatter symbols. What it has is a re-spin feature — triggered when the expanding wild lands on reels 2, 3, or 4. It can chain up to three consecutive re-spins at no cost to you.

**Q: How many paylines does Starburst have?**
Ten fixed paylines. Because of Win Both Ways, those 10 paylines evaluate winning combinations in both directions — giving you 20 active win opportunities on every spin.

**Q: Can the Starburst Wild appear on every reel?**
No. The expanding wild only lands on reels 2, 3, and 4. Reels 1 and 5 are wild-free.

**Q: What is the maximum win on Starburst?**
500x your total stake per spin. At maximum bet (R1,800 per spin), that's a potential R900,000 payout from a single spin.

**Q: What makes Starburst different from Starburst XXXtreme?**
The original: low volatility, 500x max win, re-spins only. XXXtreme: high volatility, 200,000x max win, random multipliers on wilds, paid feature buy. Same expanding wild concept — completely different risk profiles.

**Q: Is there a scatter symbol in Starburst?**
No. The Starburst Wild is the only bonus-triggering symbol. The absence of scatters is one of the things that makes Starburst genuinely distinct.

**Q: What is the RTP for Starburst?**
Standard RTP is 96.09%, above industry average. In some markets, NetEnt offers a variant reaching 99.06% — check the in-game rules at your specific casino for the applicable figure.

**Q: Is Starburst suitable for beginners?**
Absolutely. Low volatility, simple mechanics, fixed paylines, clear symbol hierarchy — Starburst is one of the most consistently recommended first slots for new players.

**Q: Can I play Starburst on mobile in South Africa?**
Yes, fully. Starburst runs on any modern Android or iOS browser — no download required. The bold, high-contrast visuals work particularly well on smartphone screens.

**Q: What is the Starburst Challenge?**
A community and promotional concept, not an official game feature. It typically involves reaching a set win target within a fixed number of spins or a specific budget. Read the full terms before participating in any casino promotion using this name.
